Connect your Volca Keys to your chosen MIDI interface.

Select that MIDI interface in the Midi Device Setup (the channel doesn't matter).

The patch will be sent to your Volca automatically when the interface is chosen.

Note: The "peak" on the filter is not MIDI addressable and so cannot be controlled from this page. Some other options are also not MIDI addressable, such as the LFO waveform type and LFO trigger sync.

Make your patch!

My preferred way to "save" your patch is by clicking "Create Sharable Patch Link" and saving the link somewhere (and don't forget to share it!). You can also save a stream of MIDI CC data which can be played back using a DAW etc. by clicking "Save Patch".

Have fun!
